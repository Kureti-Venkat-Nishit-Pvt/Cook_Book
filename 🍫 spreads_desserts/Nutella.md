# 🍫🥜 NUTELLA

---

🕒 Prep Time: 15–20 minutes  
🍽️ Servings: 1 small jar  
🌱 Type: Homemade / Healthy Spread  

---
INGREDIENTS
---
• Hazelnuts  
• Milk  
• Honey  
• Butter  
• Cocoa Powder  

---
INSTRUCTIONS
---

1️⃣ Roast the Hazelnuts  
Roast hazelnuts until fragrant and the skins loosen.

2️⃣ Peel the Hazelnuts  
Transfer roasted hazelnuts into a container.  
Close the lid and shake well until the skins peel off.

3️⃣ Blend the Base  
Add peeled hazelnuts, milk, honey, and butter into a blender.  
Blend until smooth and creamy.

4️⃣ Add Cocoa Powder  
Add cocoa powder to the creamy mixture.  
Blend again until fully combined.

---
✅ DONE! Store in Refrigerator, or Closed Jar
---
